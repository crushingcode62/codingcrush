Logical Operator

LOGICAL OPERATOR:There are three logical operators namely, AND, OR and NOT. These operators compare two or more than two conditons at a time to detemine whether a row can be selected for the output.

OPERATORS DESCRIPTION EXAMPLE
OR For the row to be selected at least one of the conditions must be true. select name from student where rollno< 10 OR rollno>20;
AND For the row to be selected all the specific conditions must be true. select name from student where rollno< 10 AND gender="M";
NOT For the row to be selected the specific condition must be false. select name from student where gender<> "F";


Example of OR Operator: Show name of the employees whose belong to department no. 101 or 103.


Example of AND Operator: Show name, id, gender of the employees whose only male employees and belong to department ni. 101


Example of NOT Operator:Show id, name, gender of the employees whose not belongs to department no. 103
